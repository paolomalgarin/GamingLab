    \section*{\textcolor{gameOrange}{In a nutshell}}
    \noindent This game merges the strategic depth of chess with the territory-painting mechanics of Splatoon on an 8x8 board where two players start in opposite corners. Each player begins with three lives and the objective is to eliminate the opponent by shooting ink, noting that a player loses one life for every turn they remain on a square covered in the opponent's color. By default, players can move one square and shoot their ink up to a distance of two squares. However, if a player is standing on a cell already covered in their own color, they gain enhanced mobility and can move to any cell touching their current position. Additionally, moving onto a friendly colored cell makes a player invisible to their opponent until they either fire a shot or move onto a square that does not match their color. Two special items spawn randomly throughout the match to change the tide of battle. Bombs will explode after a certain number of turns, coloring a cross-shaped area of length one with whatever color the bomb was sitting on at the time of detonation. The glasses must be collected by moving onto their square and provide a temporary duration during which a player can detect an invisible opponent as long as they are within shooting range.
